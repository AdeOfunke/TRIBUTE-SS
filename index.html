Olanrewaju Fasasi

The Timeless Breed called Sound Sultan

A Time line of Olanrewaju Fasasi's life:

  • 1976 - Born in Plateau, Jos in Nigeria
  • 1990s - His showbiz career began when he hosted shows in order to raise money for studio sessions
  • 1999 - Obtained a degree in Geography and regional planning from the Lagos State University (LASU)
  • 2000 - His monster hit, “Jagbajantis” was released
  • 2007 - His contract with Kennis Music expired
  • 2009 - Married his long-term girlfriend Chichi Morah, now known as Farida Fasasi
  • 2010 -Released Back from the Future and signed artists such as Karma Da Rapper, Young GreyC, Shawn and Black under Naija Ninjas, His Record Label
  • 2012 - Became a UN Ambassador for Peace in 2012, owing to his exemplary lifestyle and career
  • 2014 - Scored his major acting role in the universally acclaimed comedy movie, "Head Gone"
  • 2015 - Partnered with investors and purchased shares in the Lagos Islanders Basketball team
  • 2021 - Reportedly receiving treatment in the form of Chemotherapy from Throat cancer
  • 2021 - Dies at the age of 44.
